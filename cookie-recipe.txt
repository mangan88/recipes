Ingredients:
	- About five dollars
	- Car
	- Gas



Directions:
	1. Fill car with gas
	2. Take your dollars and drive to local convenience store or grocery store (whichever is closer)
	3. Walk to cookie aisle and decide which cookies sound good today
	4. Take your selection to a cashier at the front of the store
	5. Offer him the money you brought when he asks
	6. Drive home
	7. Enjoy cookies.
